Plugin check

An add-on is required to use Passbolt. Download it at addons.mozilla.org.

Why do I need a plugin

Passbolt requires a browser add-on to guarantee that your secret key and your master password are never accessible to any website (including passbolt.com itself). This is also the only way to guarantee that the core cryptographic libraries cannot be tampered with. Learn more
